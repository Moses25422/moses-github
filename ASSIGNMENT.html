  • Excellence

History

KCA University was established in 1989 to provide high-quality tertiary education in Kenya. Initially focused on accountancy and business programs, it has grown into a multidisciplinary university offering a wide range of courses in technology, education, and arts. With multiple campuses and a commitment to innovation, KCA continues to be a leader in higher education in East Africa.
